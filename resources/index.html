Reviews and Theses

Trapped Ion

  • Shantanu Debnath’s thesis on trapped 171Yb ion ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/A Programmable Five Qubit Quantum Computer Using Trapped Atomic Ions.pdf)), Monroe group.
  • Alexander Erhard’s thesis on trapped 40Ca ion ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Towards scalable quantum computation with trapped ions.pdf)), Blatt group.
  • Quantum dynamics of single trapped ions ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Quantum dynamics of single trapped ions.pdf)), Reviews of Modern Physics.

Quantum Network

  • Quantum networks with trapped ions ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Quantum networks with trapped ions.pdf)), Large-scale modular quantum-computer architecture with atomic memory and photonic interconnects ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Large-scale modular quantum-computer architecture with atomic memory and photonic interconnects.pdf)), quantum network.
  • Long-distance quantum communication with atomic ensembles and linear optics ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Long-distance quantum communication with atomic ensembles and linear optics.pdf)), DLCZ scheme.

Rydberg Atom Array

  • Sylvain de Léséleuc’s thesis on Rydberg atom array ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Quantum science with optical tweezer arrays of ultracold atoms and molecules.pdf)), Antoine Browaeys group.
  • Harry Levine’s thesis on Rydberg atom array ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Quantum Information Processing and Quantum Simulation with Programmable Rydberg Atom Arrays.pdf)), Lukin group.
  • Quantum science with optical tweezer arrays of ultracold atoms and molecules ([pdf](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/ReviewsAndTheses/Quantum science with optical tweezer arrays of ultracold atoms and molecules.pdf)), Generation and manipulation of Schrödinger cat states in Rydberg atom arrays (arXiv:1905.05721), review and setup of Rydberg atom arrays (optical tweezer arrays).
  • High-Fidelity Control and Entanglement of Rydberg-Atom Qubits (arXiv:1806.04682), High-fidelity entanglement and detection of alkaline-earth Rydberg atoms (arXiv:2001.04455), Rydberg atom arrays for quantum computing.

Books

  • [R. Shankar - Principles of Quantum Mechanics](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/Shankar/R. Shankar - Principles of Quantum Mechanics.pdf) and [solution](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/Shankar/sol to shankar.pdf) 个人认为写的最好的量子力学书，吊打Sakurai。世图曾有影印版，貌似绝版了（虽然我买了一本。还有solution很难找的！这些是我自己整合的，好好看好好学！
  • [Hui Zhai - Ultracold Atomic Physics](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/Ultracold/Hui Zhai - Ultracold Atomic Physics.pdf) and solution 翟荟老师的冷原子物理是我认为清华最好的物理课，好到我每节课提前半小时去坐第一排，好到这本冷原子我已经入正了（。当然这本冷原子写的也很好（配合课食用更佳。部分习题解等我写完之后上传。翟荟，男神！
  • [A. Zee - Group Theory in a Nutshell](http://home.ustc.edu.cn/~eclipse/Repository/GroupTheory/A. Zee - Group Theory in a Nutshell for Physicists.pdf) 靠着A. Zee临时速成朱姐姐群论。虽然没读过其他的群论书，但还是推荐一哈~
  • Lukin: Introduction to Quantum Information, Modern Atomic and Optical Physics II Lukin的课，无脑冲。
